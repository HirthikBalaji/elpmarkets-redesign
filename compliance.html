Compliance & Regulation

FSC

Investment Dealer License No. C111010125

Ellipsys Financial Markets (ELP Markets) is authorized, regulated, and licensed by the Financial Services Commission (FSC) of Mauritius as a Category 1 Global Business License Investment Dealer.

Segregation of Client Funds

Client security is our highest priority. Pursuant to global regulatory requirements and FSC guidelines, ELP Markets strictly implements segregated account standards:

  • Complete Isolation: Every dollar deposited by a client is held in independent trust accounts separated from operational company funds.
  • Operational Limits: Client funds are strictly prohibited from being utilized for hedging operations, corporate overhead, or credit parameters of the company.
  • Institutional Custody: We use secure banking partnerships with Tier-1 global institutions (like Standard Chartered, Absa Bank, etc.) to store client deposits safely.

External Audits & Financial Reporting

ELP Markets maintains complete accounting transparency through continuous oversight. Our operational ledgers, trade settlement values, and balance sheets are verified by external independent auditors:

  • Annual Statutory Audits: Full corporate audits conducted yearly in strict compliance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).
  • Regulatory Submissions: Monthly and quarterly reporting of capital sufficiency ratios and liquidity volumes directly to the Financial Services Commission (FSC) Mauritius.
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML): Regular auditing of our onboarding pipelines to ensure full compliance with FATF anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing rules.
